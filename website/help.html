Getting Started

Welcome to Earplugs & Memories!

This is a comprehensive concert archive documenting 35+ years of live music experiences. Browse through 1,273 concerts, explore detailed setlists for nearly every show, view concert photos, and discover statistics about artists, venues, and songs.

What Can You Do Here?

  • Browse Concerts: View all shows with dates, venues, and artists
  • Explore Setlists: See song-by-song setlists for 99% of concerts
  • View Statistics: Discover most-played songs, top artists, and venue data
  • Share Photos: Upload your concert photos (requires sign-in)
  • Add Comments: Share your concert memories (requires sign-in)
  • Submit Setlists: Help complete the archive by submitting setlist.fm links

Install Mobile App

About the App

Earplugs & Memories is available as a Progressive Web App (PWA), which means you can install it on your phone or computer for an app-like experience. Once installed, you'll get:

  • Home Screen Icon: Launch the app directly from your device
  • Full-Screen Mode: No browser bars, just the app
  • Offline Access: View cached concerts and data without internet
  • Faster Loading: Cached data loads instantly
  • Native Feel: Works just like a native iOS/Android app

Install on iPhone/iPad

  1. Open Safari (must use Safari, not Chrome) and visit earplugsandmemories.com
  2. Tap the Share button at the bottom of the screen (square with arrow pointing up)
  3. Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
  4. Give it a name (or keep "E&M Concerts") and tap "Add"
  5. The app icon will appear on your home screen
  6. Tap the icon to launch the app in full-screen mode!

Tip: You can move the icon to your home screen or dock just like any other app!

Install on Android

  1. Open Chrome and visit earplugsandmemories.com
  2. Tap the menu (three dots ) in the top-right corner
  3. Select "Install app" or "Add to Home Screen"
  4. Confirm the installation when prompted
  5. The app will be added to your home screen and app drawer
  6. Tap to launch the full-screen app!

Note: You may also see an automatic install banner at the bottom of the page!

Install on Desktop (Chrome/Edge)

  1. Open Chrome or Edge and visit earplugsandmemories.com
  2. Look for the install icon in the address bar (usually on the right side)
  3. Click the icon and select "Install"
  4. The app will open in its own window without browser tabs
  5. Access it from your taskbar, Start menu, or desktop shortcut

Alternative: Click the menu (⋮) → Settings → "Install Earplugs & Memories"

Why Install?

Installing the app gives you the best possible experience:

  • Instant loading with cached data
  • Browse concerts even when offline
  • Distraction-free full-screen interface
  • Quick access from your home screen or taskbar
  • Automatic updates when new concerts are added

Contributing to the Archive

Uploading Photos

Share your concert photos with the community!

  • Sign In Required: Create an account to upload photos
  • From Concert Page: Click "Upload Photo" button on any concert page
  • Photo Guidelines:
    • • Maximum file size: 5MB
    • • Accepted formats: JPG, PNG, GIF
    • • Photos are automatically resized for optimal loading
    • • Add an optional caption to describe the photo
  • Managing Photos: You can delete your own photos anytime

Adding Comments

Share your memories and experiences from concerts you attended.

  • Sign In Required: Create an account to post comments
  • From Concert Page: Scroll to the comments section at the bottom
  • Editing: You can edit your own comments after posting
  • Deleting: You can delete your own comments anytime

Submitting Setlists

Help complete the archive by submitting setlist.fm links for concerts without setlists.

  • No Sign-In Required: Anyone can submit setlists!
  • From Concert Page: Look for "Submit Setlist" button on concerts without setlists
  • Finding Setlists:
    • • Go to setlist.fm
    • • Search for the artist and date
    • • Copy the URL from the setlist page
    • • Paste the URL in the submission form
  • Approval Process: Submissions are reviewed by admins before being added
  • Automatic Import: Once approved, the setlist is automatically imported

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need an account to browse concerts?

No! You can browse all concerts, setlists, artists, venues, and statistics without creating an account. An account is only required to upload photos, post comments, or submit setlists.

How do I know if a concert has a setlist?

Look for the green list icon next to the concert. This indicates a complete setlist is available. Currently, 99% of concerts have setlists!

Can I download concert photos?

Yes! Click on any photo to view it full-size, then right-click and select "Save Image As" to download.

What if I find incorrect information?

Please contact us with details about the error, and we'll review and correct it as soon as possible.

How are setlists sourced?

Setlists are primarily sourced from setlist.fm, a community-driven setlist database. Some may also come from personal notes or other reliable sources.

Can I edit or delete my photos/comments?

Yes! You can edit your own comments by clicking the "Edit" button. You can delete your own photos and comments anytime by clicking the "Delete" button.

What happens after I submit a setlist?

Your submission is reviewed by an admin to verify accuracy. Once approved, the setlist is automatically imported and the concert page is updated. This usually happens within a few days.

Admin Features

This section is only visible to site administrators. These features are not available to regular users.

Adding New Concerts

Add concerts to the archive using the web form.

  • Access: Click "Add Concert" in the navigation menu (only visible when signed in as admin)
  • Required Info: Date, venue, city, state, and at least one artist
  • Multiple Artists: Add headliner and support acts (openers)
  • Festival Shows: Use the festival name field for multi-artist events
  • Auto-Processing: Concerts are automatically processed and assigned IDs
  • Artists & Venues: System automatically creates or links to existing artist/venue records

Reviewing Setlist Submissions

Review and approve user-submitted setlists from setlist.fm.

  • Access: Click "Pending Setlists" in the navigation (badge shows pending count)
  • Review Process:
    • • Check that the setlist matches the correct concert
    • • Verify the date and artist are correct
    • • Review song list for accuracy
    • • Click "Approve" to import or "Reject" to decline
  • Automatic Import: Approved setlists are automatically fetched from setlist.fm and imported
  • Auto-Deploy: After import, the website automatically updates with the new setlist

Editing Concerts

Modify concert details as needed.

  • From Concert Page: Click "Edit Concert" button (admin only)
  • Editable Fields: Date, venue, artists, festival name
  • Caution: Changing core info may break links or cause data inconsistencies

Updating Existing Setlists

Refresh or correct setlist data for concerts that already have setlists.

  • From Concert Page: Click "Update Setlist" button next to "View on Setlist.fm" (admin only)
  • When to Use:
    • • The existing setlist has errors or is incomplete
    • • You found a better or more accurate setlist on setlist.fm
    • • The setlist needs to be refreshed with updated data
    • • Multi-artist shows need all setlists added (opener + headliner)
  • How It Works:
    • • Click the "Update Setlist" button on the concert page
    • • A form will appear asking for the new setlist.fm URL
    • • Paste the complete setlist.fm URL (e.g., https://www.setlist.fm/setlist/...)
    • • Click "Update" to submit
    • • The system fetches fresh data from setlist.fm automatically
    • • The existing setlist is replaced with the new data
    • • The website automatically updates within seconds
  • Multi-Artist Shows: For co-headliners or opener + headliner shows, update with the URL that includes all performers
  • Auto-Deploy: Updates trigger automatic deployment, and the page reloads with fresh data
  • Instant Approval: Admin updates are automatically approved without review

Personal Notes

Add private notes to concerts (only visible to admins).

  • From Concert Page: Look for the "Personal Notes" section (admin only)
  • Privacy: Notes are completely private and not visible to regular users
  • Use Cases: Personal memories, special moments, planning notes
  • Editing: Click "Edit Notes" to modify anytime

Content Moderation

Manage user-contributed content.

  • Delete Photos: Remove any photo by clicking the delete button
  • Delete Comments: Remove any comment by clicking the delete button
  • Auto-Deploy: Deletions automatically trigger a site update

Backend Management

Python scripts for advanced management tasks.

  • Export Script: export_to_web.py - Exports Firestore to JSON
  • Add Admins: setup_admins.py - Manage admin users
  • Bulk Operations: Various scripts for data fixes and migrations
  • Manual Deploy: ./deploy.sh - Deploy changes manually
